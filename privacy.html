Privacy Policy

Your data should stay out of the way, too.

Last updated: April 2, 2026

1. Overview

SkipTheVid - YouTube Summarizer is a Chrome extension and website designed to help you send YouTube videos into Gemini for faster summarization and topic exploration.

We try to keep data collection minimal. We do not currently run our own user account system, our own payment system, or our own backend for storing your summaries.

2. What the extension stores

The extension stores a small amount of data in Chrome extension storage so it can work properly. This may include:

  • Your saved Gemini chat URL
  • Your topic or channel names and their Gemini chat URLs
  • Your prompt template and any channel-specific prompt overrides
  • Local usage history such as recently summarized YouTube links, timestamps, and time-saved estimates
  • UI and extension preferences such as dismissed notices, onboarding flags, and related local state

3. Where that data is stored

Some extension data is stored using chrome.storage.local, which stays on the device and browser profile where the extension is installed.

Some settings are stored using chrome.storage.sync. That means those settings may sync through the user’s Chrome account and be available across signed-in Chrome browsers, subject to Google Chrome’s own syncing behavior and privacy practices.

So the short version is: your data is not being sent to our own backend, but it is not necessarily only stored on one local machine either because Chrome sync may apply.

Your history is currently kept in extension storage and is not synced to our own servers unless we add optional backend sync in the future.

4. What gets sent to third parties

When you use the extension, it opens Gemini and inserts your chosen prompt into the Gemini interface. That prompt typically includes the YouTube video URL you want Gemini to summarize.

Once that information is submitted in Gemini, it is handled by Google under Google’s own terms and privacy practices, not ours.

The extension also reads YouTube page HTML in order to estimate video duration for the local “time saved” metric shown in the extension.

The extension may also load standard YouTube thumbnail images when showing your recent history inside the extension popup.

5. What we do not currently do

  • We do not sell your personal data.
  • We do not run our own ad network or behavioral advertising system.
  • We do not store your summaries, prompts, or usage history on our own servers.
  • We do not require you to create an account to use the extension.

6. Website data

This website is a simple marketing site. Depending on the hosting provider used, basic server logs such as IP address, browser information, and request timestamps may be collected automatically for security and delivery purposes.

We do not currently state that we use analytics, tracking pixels, or ad-tech on this site unless and until those tools are actually added.

7. Security

We take reasonable steps to keep the extension lightweight and to avoid collecting more data than needed. However, no browser, device, extension, or third-party platform can guarantee perfect security.

8. Children’s privacy

SkipTheVid - YouTube Summarizer is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we do, we will post the updated version on this page and update the “Last updated” date above.

10. Contact
