The opportunity

Meet customers at the moment they need mechanic help.

Black Thumb brings auto repair and maintenance directly to customers in Bend and across Central Oregon. Because many customers look for mechanic help at the exact moment they need it, digital visibility can become one of Black Thumb’s strongest sources of growth.

This revised launch keeps the essentials: a strong website, clear service request path, basic local search setup, and enough profile work to create a foundation for future expansion.

The revised launch package

A leaner foundation focused on website, contact flow, and basic visibility.

This version removes the heavier marketing strategy, content, review, citation, and tracking systems from the original scope. The goal is to give Black Thumb a strong foundational online presence.

Revised fixed-scope partnership package

Black Thumb Online Business Launch

Website, lead routing, basic brand positioning, basic Google Business Profile setup, social profile creation, search engine submission, analytics, website platform setup, standard first-year website hosting, professional email, and website launch.

Project investment $2,500

50% to begin, 50% at launch

01

Company & Brand Positioning

Establish a clear, friendly baseline so Black Thumb has consistent language for the website and core business profiles.

  • Discovery session covering customers, goals, service area, competitors, and business story
  • Positioning statement, core value proposition, and primary customer messages
  • Definition and presentation of up to five priority services
  • Short business description and tagline direction

02

Website & Online Engagement

Give prospective customers a fast, mobile-friendly path from a local search to a clear, organized service request.

  • Five-page website: Home, Services, Automotive Instruction, About, and Contact
  • Website structure, copy development, mobile optimization, and accessible design
  • Tap-to-call and tap-to-text actions placed throughout the customer journey
  • Request-service form capturing contact, location, vehicle, and service details
  • Form and email routing to a dedicated business inbox with clear subject lines, notifications, and tested delivery
  • Basic analytics for traffic sources, page activity, form submissions, and key contact actions
  • Initial website platform setup, domain connection support, Google Search Console, and technical launch setup
  • Standard first-year website hosting included in the launch package (renews @ $~250/year)
  • Email hosted with Gmail for 1 year included in the launch package (renews @ ~$70/year)
  • Website platform ownership and access can be transferred to Black Thumb upon request
  • Privacy Policy and Terms pages aligned with forms, analytics, and contact methods used at launch

03

Get Found & Build Basic Visibility

Create the minimum credible local presence needed for customers and search engines find Black Thumb online.

  • Basic Google Business Profile setup or optimization, including core services, photos, contact details, hours, and service area
  • Confirm or create basic Facebook, Instagram, Google, and YouTube presence as relevant to the business
  • Submit the website to search engines for initial visibility

04

Launch & Follow-Through

Make sure the system works in practice and Black Thumb can confidently share online presence after the public launch.

  • Cross-device website review and testing of every call, text, form, and email path
  • Two consolidated revision rounds
  • Launch coordination
  • Shared login information
  • 30-day post-launch analytics and lead-flow check-in
  • Recommendations for the next 90 days

This revised scope intentionally removes business email configuration, deeper local SEO research, citation creation, launch social content, reusable social content templates, formal review workflows, and lead-source tracking, and website platform training.
