The opportunity

Meet customers at the moment they need mechanic help.

Black Thumb brings auto repair and maintenance directly to customers in Bend and across Central Oregon. Because many customers look for mechanic help at the exact moment they need it, digital visibility can become one of Black Thumb’s strongest sources of growth: especially through Google Search, Google Maps, reviews, and clear calls or booking requests from the website.

This proposal is structured as a practical collaboration: Black Thumb gets a right-sized launch at partnership pricing, and Tom may document the public-facing work and results as a local business case study.

Project goals

A complete foundation without an agency-sized rollout.

Get found locally

Make Black Thumb easy to discover in Bend, Central Oregon, Google Search, and Google Maps.

Build trust quickly

Use reviews, real work photos, credentials, service process, and clear communication.

Turn interest into leads

Guide visitors toward calls, texts, quote requests, and booking conversations.

Create repeatable systems

Set up review requests, lead tracking, content habits, and monthly improvement loops.

The launch package

One coordinated foundation for launching the business online.

These pieces are designed to work together. The brand direction shapes the website and social media presence, the digital footprint turns attention into organized inquiries, and the local profiles help nearby customers find and trust Black Thumb.

Black Thumb Online Business Launch

01

Company & Brand Positioning

Establish a clear, friendly identity so Black Thumb sounds like the same trusted local business everywhere customers encounter it.

  • Discovery session covering customers, goals, service area, competitors, and business story
  • Positioning statement, core value proposition, and primary customer messages
  • Definition and presentation of up to five priority services
  • Local, friendly voice and tone guidance for Bend and Central Oregon
  • Trust points, differentiators, short business description, and tagline direction
  • Practical brand and messaging guide for future website, social, and print use

02

Website & Online Engagement

Give prospective customers a fast, mobile-friendly path from a local search to a clear, organized service request.

  • Five-page website: Home, Services, Automotive Instruction, About, and Contact
  • Website structure, copy development, mobile optimization, and accessible design
  • Tap-to-call and tap-to-text actions placed throughout the customer journey
  • Request-service form capturing contact, location, vehicle, and service details
  • Form and email routing to a dedicated business inbox with clear subject lines, notifications, and tested delivery
  • Basic analytics for traffic sources, page activity, form submissions, and key contact actions
  • Domain connection, dedicated business email setup, Google Search Console, and technical launch setup
  • 12 months of standard website hosting, SSL, backups, uptime monitoring, essential technical maintenance, and occasional reasonable text edits or photo swaps within the existing pages and layout included
  • Privacy Policy and Terms pages aligned with forms, analytics, and contact methods used at launch

03

Get Found & Build Reputation

Create a consistent local presence that helps Black Thumb appear credible in search, maps, social channels, and customer reviews.

  • Google Business Profile setup or optimization, including services, photos, Q&A, and three launch posts
  • Local keyword research and on-page SEO for priority services and the service area
  • Up to ten high-priority business listing or citation updates
  • Complete Facebook and Instagram setup with consistent profile and cover graphics and bio information
  • Six ready-to-publish launch posts plus reusable content templates
  • Google review link and QR code, customer request messages, and response templates
  • Simple lead-source tracker to connect calls, texts, forms, and referrals to marketing activity

04

Launch, Training & Follow-Through

Make sure the system works in practice and Black Thumb can use it confidently after the public launch.

  • Cross-device website review and testing of every call, text, form, and email path
  • Two consolidated revision rounds
  • Launch coordination and account handoff
  • One training session with written reference materials
  • 30-day post-launch analytics and lead-flow check-in
  • Recommendations for the next 90 days

Google controls profile verification and approval; no agency can guarantee ranking or approval. Search rankings and lead volume depend on competition, service area, review growth, budget, seasonality, and customer response. The brand and messaging guide uses Black Thumb’s existing identity as its starting point; a full logo or visual identity redesign is outside this package. First-year website care covers the technical health of the existing site plus occasional reasonable text edits and photo swaps within the existing pages and layout. New pages, new sections, layout changes, and ongoing marketing work are separate. Domain registration and business email licenses remain client-owned and are paid directly by Black Thumb.
